# Past: Reclaiming Power & Moving Forward

## What This Does

Takes everything you've learned in the first two past-focused skills and helps you distill lessons, release what no longer serves, reclaim your power, and make a conscious declaration about who you're choosing to become.

## Output

Saves to: `my-brain/02_Wiki/campaign/03-release-and-reclaim.md`

## Command Protocol

- **help** / **ask llm** — Get AI guidance on the current question
- **save** — Save progress and exit (resume later)
- **skip** — Skip current question
- **review** — Show all answers so far for editing
- **back** — Go to previous question
- **done** — Finalize and generate output

## Questions

### Lessons from the Past

1. Looking back at what you've explored about your childhood, identity, and inner blocks — what are the most important lessons you take from your past?

2. What has your past taught you about what you truly need to thrive? (Not what you were told you should need.)

3. What patterns do you now see clearly that you want to keep? What strengths were forged in difficulty?

### What You're Ready to Release

4. What are you holding onto that no longer serves you? (Beliefs, resentments, stories, identities, relationships.)

5. What tasks or roles drain you and feel out of alignment with your true self?

6. What parts of your current self are you ready to leave behind? (The version of you that plays small, that waits for permission, that avoids the hard thing.)

7. If your life stayed exactly the same for the next 5 years, what would you regret not changing?

### Reclaiming Power

8. What activities, environments, or types of work make you feel most alive and authentic?

9. What power do you reclaim today? What did you give away that you're taking back?

10. What's one truth about yourself that you've been avoiding that you're ready to face?

11. What would you do today if fear, judgment, or failure didn't matter?

### Moving Forward: Declaration

12. Who are you choosing to become? Write a short declaration of intent — a sentence or paragraph that captures the shift you're making.

13. What one belief, behavior, or habit are you ready to leave behind — starting now?

14. What's the first action you'll take as this new version of yourself?
