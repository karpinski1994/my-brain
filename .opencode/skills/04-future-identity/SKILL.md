# Future Self: Identity & Emotions

## What This Does

Takes you through envisioning the most aligned, powerful version of yourself — who they are at their core, what they value, how they think, and how they feel. This creates the emotional compass for your transformation.

## Output

Saves to: `my-brain/02_Wiki/campaign/04-future-identity-emotions.md`

## Command Protocol

- **help** / **ask llm** — Get AI guidance on the current question
- **save** — Save progress and exit (resume later)
- **skip** — Skip current question
- **review** — Show all answers so far for editing
- **back** — Go to previous question
- **done** — Finalize and generate output

## Questions

### Identity & Self-Image

1. How would you describe the most aligned, powerful version of yourself in three sentences or less? Imagine this person has already achieved your goals and lives in integrity with their values.

2. What are three core values or principles this version of you lives by — no matter what?

3. What character traits does this future self embody, even under pressure? (e.g., calm, focused, honest, consistent, courageous.)

4. What words do people use to describe this version of you? What reputation do they have?

5. What do they believe about themselves — their capabilities, their worth, their potential?

6. What's their relationship with themselves like? How do they treat themselves on a hard day?

7. What identity shifts are happening? Who are they becoming that they weren't before?

### Emotional & Mental State

8. How does this future self feel most of the time — internally? Describe their emotional baseline. (Examples: calm, motivated, centered, joyful, clear-headed, peaceful.)

9. How do they respond to failure, conflict, or setbacks? What's their default reaction?

10. What inner dialogue replaces old self-criticism? What do they tell themselves when things get hard?

11. What fears or limiting beliefs no longer control this version of you?

12. What is this version of you no longer afraid to say, do, or ask for?

13. What emotions do you want more of in your daily life? What emotional patterns are you leaving behind?

14. What gives this version of you a deep sense of peace and fulfillment?
