# Future Self: Daily Life & Environment

## What This Does

Maps out the practical, observable dimensions of your ideal future self — the skills they have, the habits they practice, the environment they live in, the people around them, and what their day actually looks like.

## Output

Saves to: `my-brain/02_Wiki/campaign/05-future-life-environment.md`

## Command Protocol

- **help** / **ask llm** — Get AI guidance on the current question
- **save** — Save progress and exit (resume later)
- **skip** — Skip current question
- **review** — Show all answers so far for editing
- **back** — Go to previous question
- **done** — Finalize and generate output

## Questions

### Skills & Capabilities

1. What key skills, strengths, or qualities does this version of you consistently use to get results?

2. What are they learning or studying? What knowledge are they building?

3. What one skill, if mastered, would have the greatest positive impact on their life and goals?

### Habits & Daily Routines

4. What habits are part of their daily or weekly routine — physically, mentally, emotionally? List habits related to productivity, relationships, health, creativity.

5. What does their morning routine look like? How do they start the day?

6. What does their evening routine look like? How do they wind down?

7. What health and fitness practices do they maintain?

### What They've Freed Themselves From

8. What distractions, compulsions, or time-wasters does this future self no longer engage in? What are they free from?

9. What do they love doing that the current version of you tends to avoid or procrastinate?

### Environment

10. Where do they live? What does their home look like and feel like?

11. What kind of physical environment (home, workspace, digital space) supports this version of you? Describe the ideal setup.

12. What does financial freedom mean to this version of you? How much do they earn, save, and invest?

### Relationships

13. What kind of people surround this version of you? Who are their key relationships?

14. What kind of partner, friend, family member are they? How do they show up for others?

15. Who are they no longer giving time or energy to? What boundaries do they maintain?

### A Day in the Life

16. Describe a typical day in the life of this version of you — from morning to night. Walk through the whole day.

17. What are they focused on building or creating long-term? Why does it matter to them?

18. What goals is this version of you committed to — and what's their attitude toward achieving them?

### Final Integration

19. If you were to fully step into this version of yourself today, what would you do differently in the next 24 hours?

20. What's one belief, behavior, or habit you are ready to leave behind — starting now?
