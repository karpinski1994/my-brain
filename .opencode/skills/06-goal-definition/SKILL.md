# Goal: Clarify & Define Goals

## What This Does

Takes you through the first critical steps of goal-setting: deciding exactly what you want in every key area of life, writing goals down clearly, setting deadlines, and clarifying your dream into a specific, measurable target.

## Questions

### Step 1: Idealize — No Limits

1. Imagine there are no limitations on what you can be, have, or do. You have all the time, money, connections, education, and experience you need. If you could wave a magic wand and make your life perfect in every way:

   - **Income**: How much do you want to earn this year, next year, and five years from today?

   - **Family & Lifestyle**: What kind of lifestyle do you want to create for yourself?

   - **Health**: How would your health be different if it was perfect?

   - **Net Worth**: How much do you want to save and accumulate in your lifetime?

2. The Three Goal Method: In less than 30 seconds, write down your three most important goals in life right now. Write quickly. Whatever comes out first is probably your truth.

### Step 2: Clarify Your Dream

3. Visualize the life you want to live in 5 years. Where do you live? What does your ideal day look like from morning to night? How do you feel in your body, mind, and relationships? What are you most proud of having accomplished?

4. Write this as if it already happened: "It's [date 5 years from now]. I wake up in ______. Each morning I ______. I feel proud because ______."

5. Why do you want this? What would it mean to you to achieve this vision?

### Step 3: Define It Clearly

6. Turn your dream into measurable goals across key areas:

   - **Health & Fitness** (e.g., run 10km, lose weight, gain muscle, specific benchmarks)

   - **Learning & Skills** (e.g., master a language, develop a skill, get certified)

   - **Finances** (specific savings, income, debt freedom numbers)

   - **Environment** (where you live, how your space looks, what you own)

   - **Career / Mission** (what you're building, who you serve)

   - **Experiences** (travel, adventures, things you want to do)

   - **Relationships** (what your connections look like)

7. Make each goal SMART: Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound. Write each goal in the present tense as if already achieved (e.g., "I earn $X by December 2027").

### Step 4: Write It All Down

8. Write every goal clearly in detail. Describe them as if placing an order. Be specific: numbers, dates, descriptions, feelings.

9. Begin each goal with "I" and use an action verb. Example: "I earn $150,000 per year by December 2027."

10. From your list of goals, which one goal — if you could achieve any one in 24 hours — would have the greatest positive impact on your life? That's your Major Definite Purpose.

### Step 5: Set Deadlines

11. What is your master deadline for your Major Definite Purpose?

12. Break it into sub-deadlines: yearly → monthly → weekly milestones.

13. What's the first milestone and its deadline?
