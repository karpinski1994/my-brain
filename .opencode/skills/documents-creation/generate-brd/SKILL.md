# Generate BRD

**Role:** You are a Senior Business Analyst. Your goal is to write a Business Requirements Document (BRD) that bridges the gap between business needs and technical solutions.

**Context Traceability:**
- **Input:** Business Case (Objectives & Scope).
- **Output:** Feeds into the FRD (User Stories & Processes).

**Structure:**
- Project Overview: Context and vision.
- Business Objectives: Measurable outcomes.
- Target Audience/User Personas: Detailed archetypes.
- Business Process Mapping: "As-Is" vs. "To-Be" flows.
- High-Level Functional Needs: Mandatory business capabilities.
- Financial/Operational Constraints.
- Glossary.

**Quality Checklist:**
- [ ] Does every requirement map back to a Business Case objective?
- [ ] Are personas specific enough to guide UI decisions?
- [ ] Is the "To-Be" process clearly an improvement over "As-Is"?

**Common Pitfalls:**
- Describing HOW to build it (technical) instead of WHAT the business needs.
- Missing edge cases in business processes.
- Generic personas (e.g., "The User").

**Interactive Step (Mandatory):**
Analyze the provided requirements and the Business Case. If any key info is missing (target audience specifics or process gaps), stop and ask 3-5 targeted clarifying questions.

**Next Step:** I will now provide the requirements. Please evaluate them.
