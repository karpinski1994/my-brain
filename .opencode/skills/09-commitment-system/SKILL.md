# Commitment: Discipline, Environment & Energy

## What This Does

Covers the final critical layers from Brian Tracy steps 11-12 and the worksheet's steps 7-12: developing unshakeable self-discipline, building mental conditioning practices, resetting your physical/digital environment, managing energy, and making retreat impossible.

## Questions

### Step 1: Self-Discipline Habit

1. What's your #1 keystone habit — the one habit that, if you master it, makes everything else easier?

2. Resolve to concentrate single-mindedly on your most important task until it's 100% complete. What does "single-handling" look like for you? How will you avoid context-switching?

3. What accountability system will keep you consistent? (Buddy, coach, public commitment, streak tracker, consequences.)

4. What's your plan for when motivation fades? (Because it will. Discipline > motivation.)

### Step 2: Build Mental Fortress

5. What mental conditioning practices will you install? (Journaling, cold showers, meditation, breathwork, affirmations.)

6. Write 2-3 identity affirmations: "I am someone who ______." (e.g., "I am someone who finishes what they start," "I am someone who takes action despite fear.")

7. How will you handle doubt when it shows up? What will you remind yourself?

8. What discomforts will you practice daily to build resilience?

### Step 3: Practice Visualization

9. Create a clear, vivid, exciting mental picture of your goal as if already achieved. Close your eyes and describe what you see, hear, and feel.

10. What emotions accompany the successful achievement of your goal? Take a moment to feel them fully.

11. When and where will you practice visualization daily? (e.g., first thing in the morning, before bed, during meditation.)

12. Create a trigger: what will remind you to visualize? (Alarm, after brushing teeth, sticky note on mirror.)

### Step 4: Reset Environment

13. What physical space changes do you need to make? (Clean room/desk, create deep work zone, reduce clutter.)

14. What digital environment changes? (Phone layout, app blockers, notification settings, social media cleanup.)

15. What temptations do you need to remove? What visual reminders of your goals do you need to add?

16. Control the environment → Control your behavior. What's the #1 environmental change you'll make today?

### Step 5: Guard Energy & Identity

17. What drains your energy? (People, activities, information, environments — list them.)

18. What energizes you? (What fills your battery back up?)

19. How many hours of sleep do you need? What's your sleep plan?

20. What inputs will you limit? (Junk information, doomscrolling, bad news, gossip, negative conversations.)

21. What decisions will you automate to save willpower? (Meals, clothes, routines.)

22. High performance = consistency + recovery. What's your recovery plan?

### Step 6: Make Mission Unavoidable

23. What internal consequences will you set? (e.g., "If I skip this, I do X pushups." "If I miss a day, I donate $Y to a cause I hate.")

24. What external consequences? (Public commitment, tell someone your goal, post progress.)

25. Tie identity to behavior: what's one sentence that summarizes who you're becoming?

26. Burn the escape routes. What can you do to make retreat more painful than pushing forward?
