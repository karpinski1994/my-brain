# Past: Identity, Shadows & Inner Blocks

## What This Does

Explores your current self-image, strengths and limitations, shadow traits, inner critic patterns, emotional baggage, and the self-sabotage cycles that keep you stuck.

## Output

Saves to: `my-brain/02_Wiki/campaign/02-identity-shadows-blocks.md`

## Command Protocol

- **help** / **ask llm** — Get AI guidance on the current question
- **save** — Save progress and exit (resume later)
- **skip** — Skip current question
- **review** — Show all answers so far for editing
- **back** — Go to previous question
- **done** — Finalize and generate output

## Questions

### Self-Understanding & Identity

1. How would you describe your current self-image in a few clear sentences? Who do you believe you are right now?

2. What are three things you believe define who you are right now — your roles, labels, or core traits?

3. Which parts of your identity feel chosen vs inherited? What did you consciously decide about who you are, and what did you absorb from others?

### Life Situation & Behavior

4. What is your current living situation and financial reality? (Be honest — this is your baseline.)

5. What daily habits or routines feel most familiar to you — both helpful and harmful?

6. Which habits feel aligned with who you want to become? Which feel like they're holding you back?

7. What's the gap between where you are and where you want to be? In work, relationships, health, finances?

### Strengths & Shadows

8. What are five inner strengths or qualities you rely on most?

9. What do others praise you for? What do they come to you for?

10. What are five recurring patterns or limitations you've noticed in yourself?

11. What do you often judge in others — and what might that reflect about parts of yourself you haven't accepted?

12. What are your "shadows" — traits you suppress, hide, or feel ashamed of?

### Self-Talk & Inner Critic

13. What negative thoughts or self-criticisms show up regularly in your mind?

14. When is your inner critic loudest? What triggers it?

15. What's the most common fear or doubt that tends to surface when you think about going after what you want?

16. How do you self-protect? What walls, excuses, or avoidance strategies do you use?

### Emotional Baggage

17. What emotions — such as anger, shame, guilt, or sadness — still feel unresolved in your body?

18. Are there people from your past (or present) you still feel resentment or blame toward?

19. Where do you feel inner conflict about what you want? What parts of you are pulling in different directions?

### Self-Sabotage

20. What does self-sabotage look like for you? When do you stop yourself from moving forward?

21. When you avoid something important, what do you tell yourself? How do you justify it?

22. What thoughts or fears regularly prevent you from taking action on what matters most?

23. What's one fear or belief you know isn't rational — but still feels true in your body?

24. What would you do if you knew you couldn't fail?
