# Psychological Portrait

A comprehensive psychological profile covering ADHD assessment, personality type evolution, and deep personal history.

## Summary

The portrait reveals a complex, evolving personality: from ENFP (creative, emotional) through ENTJ (action/management) and ISTJ (discipline/system) to INTP (reflective analyst). Key struggles include fear of visibility, ADHD tendencies (score 3/6 on ASRS), gaming addiction (LoL as dopamine escape), deep-rooted childhood patterns (father's fear of "sticking out", mockery from peers), and business self-doubt despite positive results.

## Key Claims

- ADHD self-report scored 3/6 on ASRS (threshold is 4) — just below clinical threshold but showing significant tendencies (source: `01_Raw/ingested/everything - my psychological portrait.md`)
- Personality evolution: ENFP (2019) → ENTJ (2021) → ISTJ (2022) → INTP (2025) — showing deliberate adaptation and skill-building (source: same)
- Core insight: "You are an architect, not a bricklayer" — value lies in designing solutions, not executing individual tasks (source: same)
- Core reframe: "Document, don't create" — reduce pressure of ideal content creation (source: same)

## Key Patterns Identified

- **Fear of Visibility**: Father's "don't stick out" mindset + childhood mockery of videos → adult fear of marketing
- **Dopamine Escape**: LoL addiction as predictable low-energy relief from anxiety
- **Entrepreneurial Doubt**: "Marketing is a scam" belief clashes with running a marketing business
- **ADHD Tendencies**: Procrastination, task avoidance, difficulty with organization, emotional dysregulation
- **Identity Conflict**: Creative soul (ENFP/INTP) trapped in execution role (ISTJ)
