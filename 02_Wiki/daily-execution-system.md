# Daily Execution System

The daily habits, discipline blocks, and focus techniques that turn goals into daily action.

## Summary

Success comes from consistent daily execution. Key practices: non-negotiable deep work blocks, single-tasking on the most important task, habit tracking, focus/concentration, and designing your environment for success. Start every day with your hardest, highest-leverage task.

## Key Claims

- Focus and concentration are the keys to success — know exactly what you want and dedicate yourself only to those actions (source: `01_Raw/12-Step-Goal-Setting-Guide.md`)
- Single-handling: start one task, avoid all distractions, stay until it's 100% complete (source: `01_Raw/12-Step-Goal-Setting-Guide.md`)
- The 80/20 Rule: 80% of results come from 20% of activities (source: `01_Raw/12-Step-Goal-Setting-Guide.md`)
- Non-negotiable work blocks (e.g. 8-10 AM = deep work), no phone, no social media, timer for 90 minutes (source: `01_Raw/worksheet-forming-plan.md`)

## Daily Discipline Blocks

- Use non-negotiable work blocks with timer (90 min focus)
- No phone, no social media during blocks
- Focus on one single task (single-handling)
- Start with the hardest/highest-leverage task first
- Use apps: Forest, Cold Turkey, Freedom to block distractions

## Key Habits

- Wake up early
- Read 30 min/day
- Exercise 5x/week
- Meditate 10 min/day
- Study/practice new skill 60 min/day
- Journal every night
- Morning affirmations of identity
