# Personal Gamification System

A tailored gamification system designed for an INTP personality with ADHD tendencies, gaming addiction (LoL), and entrepreneurial blocks.

## Summary

The system moves beyond simple checklists to "Human-Focused Design" that accounts for specific psychological triggers. Core strategies: narrative reframing (adopting a "Secret Identity" as a Hero/Strategist), weighted XP economy (3x for high-resistance tasks), Black Hat to White Hat transitions (temporary stakes → lasting meaning), and environment design (adding friction to escapes).

## Key Strategies

### 1. Narrative Reframing
- Adopt "Secret Identity" as Hero/Strategist to observe stress objectively
- "I am stressed" → "My character has a Confusion debuff"
- Stop calling boring tasks by their real name — use narrative wrappers

### 2. High-Resistance Point Economy
- Subtitle editing = 3x XP of enjoyable tasks
- "First Strike" buff: double XP for completing first video before checking LoL
- Polynomial XP curve: `TotalXP = 100 × (Level-1)^1.5`

### 3. Black Hat to White Hat Transition
- Currently stuck in Black Hat loop (gaming for anxiety relief)
- Use temporary Black Hat pressure (stickK money stakes, anti-charity)
- Transition to White Hat meaning (Epic Meaning, Visible Progress)

### 4. Environment Design
- Physical triggers: post-it note over monitor "Cost: 5 Minute Caption Polish"
- Habit stacking: anchor work to pre-existing triggers
- Forest app to block LoL distractions (tree dies if you switch)

### 5. INTP-Friendly Tools
- BeeDone: AI "Game Master" creates dynamic challenges for Flow state
- Forest: Block distractions with growing trees
- stickK: Commitment contracts with anti-charity stakes
- Custom Notion workspace: track attributes, XP, and rewards
