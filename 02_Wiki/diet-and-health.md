# Diet and Health

Personal health profile, diet preferences, medical history, and sleep issues.

## Summary

31-year-old male, 77kg, 174cm, with desk job (programming). Main health issues: gastritis/reflux, IBS tendencies, sleep maintenance insomnia (waking multiple times nightly, not falling asleep), chronic stress, elevated bilirubin (Gilbert's suspected), back pain between shoulder blades. Diet preferences: Asian cuisine, rice-based dishes, Thai soup, salmon, chicken, minimal gas-producing foods. Previous diet was a structured meal plan by a nutritionist.

## Key Health Issues

- **Sleep**: Wakes up multiple times nightly, sometimes cannot sleep entire night; chronic stress cycle (stress → poor sleep → more stress)
- **Digestive**: Gastritis, reflux, sensitive stomach, loose stools, bloating/gas, potential IBS
- **Musculoskeletal**: Back pain between shoulder blades (non-muscular per physiotherapists)
- **Mental Health**: High chronic stress, anxiety, irritability, cognitive problems (frontal cortex less active on SPECT scan)
- **Sleep History**: Previously took Stilnox (helped temporarily), currently Duloxetine + Pregabaline, Ashwagandha, Tryptophan, Magnesium

## Diet Principles

- Easy and fast meals preferred
- Good for stress and sleep disorders
- First meal should be protein-fat based (avoid simple carbs)
- No eating 2-3 hours before sleep
- High-protein breakfast, no simple carbs
- 2 liters water daily
- 25-30g fiber daily (wheat bran 2-3 tbsp)

## Food Preferences

- **Likes**: Chicken, fish (especially salmon), rice, pasta, Asian dishes, Mexican (spicy), olive oil, butter
- **Dislikes**: Gas-producing foods (beans, onion, garlic, leek), pork, avocado, oatmeal, eggs, organ meats
- **Recipes**: Thai soup, spaghetti, shakes, sushi rice with salmon
