# Cybernetics: Social Power and Information Warfare

The Accumulator, Free Power as strategic autonomy, and cybernetic mechanisms of social control (infowiruses, normoviruses).

## Summary

Free Power = the surplus energy remaining after maintaining existence and working to sustain it. Free Power is the only energy that can be used for self-steering, growth, and adaptation. The history of exploitation is one system eliminating another's Free Power. Modern geopolitics is a cybernetic battle over Free Power.

## The Three Types of Power (Moc)

1. **Idle Power (Moc Jałowa)**: Energy to maintain existence (heartbeat, company overhead)
2. **Work Power (Moc Robocza)**: Energy to obtain Idle Power (going to work, productive activity, taxes)
3. **Free Power (Moc Swobodna)**: Surplus after 1+2 — the only energy for self-steering, goal-setting, growth, adaptation

## Critical Insight

**Free Power = Freedom.** Without Free Power, a system can only react to its environment — it cannot set its own direction. Historical exploitation (slavery, feudalism, wage extraction) = one system eliminating another's Free Power.

## Modern Applications

- China's rare earth export restrictions, US tariffs = cybernetic moves to increase adversaries' Work Power and reduce Free Power
- AI allows transition from Steerable → Self-Steering workflows, freeing humans to act as pure Homeostats

## Infowiruses and Normoviruses

**Infovirus**: A conditioned stimulus-response link injected via media/culture that triggers a reflex before rational thought. Operates between external stimulus and early internal state.

**Normovirus**: A deeper infection that alters internal rules of thumb — shaping what a person considers normal, possible, or desirable. Socialization is a packet of normoviruses.

### Healthy vs Predatory Socialization
- **Healthy**: Develops talent
- **Predatory**: Redirects energy away from talent to serve external organizer's goals (used by colonial powers, manipulative institutions)
