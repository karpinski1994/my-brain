## Daily Execution System

### Time Blocks (flexible for client meetings)

| Time | Activity |
|------|----------|
| 7-9am | Wake, move, breakfast, prep |
| 9-10am | 🐸 **Eat the frog** (#1 hardest task) |
| 10-12pm | Deep work (content, funnel, video-review) |
| 12-1pm | Break / lunch |
| 1-3pm | Shallow work (messages, approvals, automations, edits) |
| 3-5pm | Learning / US market prep |
| 5-6pm | Wrap up, plan tomorrow, **clock out** |

### Daily Non-Negotiables

1. Eat the frog (#1 hardest task) before 10am
2. Write 1 post (LinkedIn or Facebook)
3. Funnel check — 10 min review of monitoring system
4. US market prep — 30 min (course, research)
5. Plan tomorrow — 5 min at end of day
6. Clock out at fixed time + prep for next day

### Tools

- **Calendar:** Google Calendar
- **Habit tracker:** Habitica
- **Notes:** Handwriting (notepad) + Freeform on iPad
- **Weekly review:** Handwriting in notepad (2 min, Sunday)
