# Mental Fortress

Mental conditioning practices that build resilience, discipline, and the ability to push through doubt, delay, and fear.

## Summary

A set of daily mental conditioning practices that train you to handle obstacles, maintain self-discipline, and build the identity of someone who finishes what they start.

## Key Practices

- **Journaling every night** — reflect on the day, what worked, what didn't
- **Cold showers / short discomforts** — train resilience through voluntary discomfort
- **Meditation or breathwork** — build focus and emotional regulation
- **Affirmations of identity** — "I am someone who finishes what they start"

## Principles

- Guard your energy and identity: sleep 7-8 hours, automate routines to save willpower
- Limit inputs: remove junk information (doomscrolling, bad news, gossip)
- High performance comes from consistency + recovery
- Control the environment → Control your behavior
- Burn the escape routes — make your mission unavoidable
