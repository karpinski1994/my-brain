Variable & Meta Rewards
To keep the system exciting (Core Drive 7: Unpredictability), you can add mechanics that aren't just "buying" items:
The D20 Roll: Upon completing a "boring" task, roll a 20-sided die. A "Nat 20" could grant a "Legendary Reward" (like your LoL session) for free [188, Conversation History].
Dopamine Menu randomizer: Write your rewards on slips of paper and use "scratch-off stickers" to hide them, making the reward itself a surprise
.
The "Dodge" Roll: Use earned XP or a die roll to "dodge" a penalty if you miss a daily habit, effectively acting as an "emergency health potion"
.
Environmental Buffs: Some users reward themselves by "leveling up" their workspace, like buying a small indoor plant after hitting a hydration goal
.
Important Rule: Your shop must operate on a strict exchange of productivity and reward. If you want something indulgent (like gaming), you must complete a "sucky" task (like editing 5 minutes of captions) first
