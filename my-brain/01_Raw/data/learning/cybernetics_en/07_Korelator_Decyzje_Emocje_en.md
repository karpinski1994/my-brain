# Cybernetics #7: What Determines Your Decisions and Emotions? The Answer is the CORRELATOR

**Speakers:** Marcin Kapusta and Zenon Dominik Dudek

## What is the Correlator?
The **Correlator** is the functional organ responsible for processing information and codes. It is one of the core components of an autonomous system (alongside the Accumulator and the Homeostat). It connects the abstract world of information with the physical world of decisions and action.

In the human body, the correlator is most closely associated with the brain, the entire nervous system, and the hormonal system. Sensory nerves collect impressions from inside and outside the body (input), and motor nerves deliver commands to muscles and organs (output). The correlator is what sits in between—turning raw sensory input into decisions or emotional signals.

**The Correlator's Outputs:**
1. **Decision (Decyzja):** A resolved output that, when combined with sufficient energy (from the Accumulator), drives a physical action via the Effector.
2. **Emotion (Emocja):** A signal sent to the Homeostat (the goal-maintaining organ), communicating whether the current situation aligns with or threatens the system's fundamental equilibrium. Emotions are not irrational noise—they are information about the state of the system's core function.

## The Structure of the Correlator
The Correlator consists of four types of potential (nodes) connected by pathways of correlative conductivity:
1. **Receptor Potential:** Input from sensory impressions from the environment.
2. **Homeostat Potential:** Input from internal reflections—the "inner voice" or goal-sense from the Homeostat.
3. **Estimatory Potential:** Output responsible for generating decisions.
4. **Perturbatory Potential:** Output responsible for generating emotions/signals of disturbance.

## Rigid Parameters: Intelligence, Processing Speed, and Talent
The Correlator has **rigid (fixed) parameters** that define its structural architecture:
1. **Intelligence (Inteligencja):** The number of correlative nodes (neurons). A larger number of nodes means a larger capacity for storing and associating information.
2. **Processing Speed / Aptitude (Pojętność):** The speed at which information flows between nodes—how quickly a person "grasps" something.
3. **Talent:** The uneven distribution of these nodes and connections across different areas. Talent is the "highways" of the brain—the pathways that are densely connected and allow for effortless, high-speed processing in a specific domain.

Talent is **inherited** and **fixed**. Trying to fight it (by forcing someone to be good at something they have no neural architecture for) is like charging a toll to drive on your own highway—you can block access to your best routes, but you can't build new ones with the same efficiency.

The Cybernetic definition of **happiness (szczęście)** is: utilizing your unique talent in an environment that provides appropriate positive feedback for it.

## Infowiruses and Normowiruses: The Tools of Informational Steering
A key theme of this episode is how external organizers (states, corporations, media) can systematically alter the correlators of entire populations. Two key mechanisms exist:

### Infovirus (Infowirus)
An infovirus is a package of information injected into a society (via media, education, popular culture) that creates an artificial conditioned connection between an input (a stimulus/impression) and an internal reaction. Like Pavlov's dog, where a bell was conditioned to trigger salivation, an infovirus conditions a human to reflexively react to a specific stimulus (e.g., a word, an image, an idea) with fear, disgust, or uncritical approval—**before any rational reflection occurs**.

Infowiruses operate **between input impressions and internal state**—they hijack the early stages of perception.

### Normovirus (Normowirus)
A normovirus goes deeper. It installs artificial "rules" at the level of **reflexes, imagination, and consciousness**—between the nodes *inside* the correlator. A normovirus doesn't just trigger a reflex to an outside stimulus; it shapes the internal landscape of what a person considers normal, possible, or desirable.

Together, infowiruses and normowiruses form **socialization**—the process by which a child is shaped by its environment. Healthy socialization uses these mechanisms to help a child understand and develop their unique talent. Predatory socialization (used by colonial or imperial powers) uses these mechanisms to make people work against their own interests and talent, redirecting their potential to serve an external organizer.

## The IQ Myth
Standard IQ tests are criticized as "pseudo-information" because they conflate the three rigid parameters (intelligence, aptitude, and talent) in unknown proportions, and then only test a tiny slice of talent (primarily logical-mathematical). This tells us almost nothing real about a person's full cognitive capacity and can be actively misleading.

## The Correlator in Organizations
In a business, the correlator function is performed by:
- **Reception Potential:** Market researchers, client-facing staff gathering external signals.
- **Homeostat Potential / Reflections:** Management and R&D teams translating goals into resource plans.
- **Estimatory Potential (Decisions):** Sales teams processing orders, logistics scheduling.
- **Perturbatory Potential (Emotions → Signals):** Team leads and managers flagging issues upward that require strategic-level decisions.

Just as individual correlators can be infected with normowiruses, companies can also be unconsciously "infected" by external regulations, market manipulations, or media narratives that steer their decision-making in directions that serve external organizers rather than the company's own interests.
