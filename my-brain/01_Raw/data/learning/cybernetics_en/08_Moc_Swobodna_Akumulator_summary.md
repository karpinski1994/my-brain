# Summary: Cybernetics #8 - Free Power and the Accumulator

## Key Concepts
*   **Accumulator:** The energo-material functional organ that stores and distributes energy/potential to all other organs of the system (Receptor, Correlator, Homeostat, Effector). Analogous to blood in the human body or a battery in electronics.
*   **Three Types of Power (Moc):**
    1. *Idle Power (Moc Jałowa):* Energy consumed purely to maintain the system's existence (heartbeat, breathing; company overheads).
    2. *Work Power (Moc Robocza):* Energy spent to *obtain* Idle Power from the environment (going to work to earn food; productive business activity). Taxes are classified here.
    3. *Free Power (Moc Swobodna):* **The surplus remaining after the above two.** This is the only energy that can be used for self-steering — setting goals, growing, developing, and adapting the environment to one's own parameters.

## The Critical Insight: Free Power is Freedom
*   **Cybernetic Happiness:** The alignment of a system's character with its environment. Free Power is what enables a system to make that alignment happen.
*   **Historical Power Dynamics:** The entire history of exploitation (slavery, feudalism, modern wage extraction) can be understood as one system systematically eliminating another system's Free Power, forcing all of its energy into Work Power, leaving nothing for autonomous self-steering.
*   **Modern Geopolitics:** China's rare earth export restrictions, US port tariffs — these are all cybernetic moves to increase adversaries' Work Power and reduce their Free Power (strategic autonomy).
