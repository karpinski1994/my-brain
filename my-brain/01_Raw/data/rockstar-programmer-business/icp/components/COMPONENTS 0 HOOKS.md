This audience sees themselves as senior engineers trapped in a rigged market: overqualified, underpaid, hidden behind agencies and ATS filters. They don’t lack skill—they lack direct access, leverage, and a clear, low-risk path to play on the global field where their work is already valued.  
 The dominant emotional tension is “I know I’m worth more, but stability \+ middlemen keep me stuck.”

The research reveals a demographic trapped in a paradox: they are highly skilled "Hidden Giants" (Senior Engineers, Tech Leads, Architects) who are treated as "Cheap Resources" by the local outsourcing market.
