## A Typical Day

### The Designed Ideal Day

**06:30-07:00** — Wake naturally. Sunlight on terrace. 5min meditation. No phone.

**07:00-07:30** — Light movement (stretch, swim, walk). Cold shower optional. Tea/coffee.

**07:30-08:00** — Light breakfast (prepped yesterday, no heavy carbs, no food coma). Review **top 3 priorities** — nothing else exists.

**08:00-10:30** — **Deep work block.** The hardest, most important thing. Product, strategy, content planning, sales. Phone in another room. No notifications.

**10:30-11:00** — Break. Walk outside. No screens.

**11:00-12:00** — People time. Client call, team sync, mentoring session. Social, energizing.

**12:00-12:30** — Lunch. Light, prepped. No heavy carbs. Read while eating.

**12:30-13:30** — Learning / exploration. Read, study AI, expand thinking. No pressure to produce.

**13:30-14:30** — Shallow work. Emails, approvals, quick decisions, reviews.

**14:30-16:00** — **Free time. No guilt.** Pool, nap, explore, play, call a friend. Prevents evening escape spiral.

**16:00-17:00** — Movement. Gym, run, swim — whatever feels good. State reset.

**17:00-18:30** — Relationship time. Present, no phone. Walk, cook, talk.

**18:30-19:30** — Dinner. Good food, good conversation. No work talk.

**19:30-20:30** — Passion / creative time. Write, plan big ideas, learn something fun. Only what you *want* to do.

**20:30-21:00** — Prepare tomorrow. Set 3 priorities. Prep meals. Tidy workspace.

**21:00-21:30** — Wind down. Journal one sentence. Read fiction. No screens.

**21:30** — Sleep.

### Why This Works

| Challenge | Solution Built In |
|-----------|-------------------|
| Bad start = bad day | Protected morning deep work block |
| Food coma kills afternoon | Light breakfast + lunch |
| Evening escape spiral | Free time *built in* so you don't rebel |
| Hates boring tasks | Editing delegated; shallow work timeboxed |
| Rebel hates structure | Flexible — you choose within it |
| Needs adventure/variety | Afternoon learning + free time + passion block |
| Relationship fighting | Dedicated relationship time, no phones |
| Can't recover from interruption | Phone away, notifications off during deep work |
