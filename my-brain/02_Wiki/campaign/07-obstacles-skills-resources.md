### Failure Reasons (Q6)

>

### Obstacle Prevention System (Q7) — Gamified, Rebel-Friendly

**Environment Design (no willpower needed):**
- Second phone / old phone — only work apps. Main phone in drawer during deep work.
- Separate macOS user account with zero distractions.
- Website blockers for deep work hours (use them, they work when you choose them).

**Gamification System:**
- Streak tracker for deep work hours.
- XP system: deep work = XP, delegation = XP, no games before 5PM = XP. Spend on guilt-free game time.
- Boss Battle: each week's revenue target hit = unlock reward.

**Sleep as a weapon:**
- Same wake time daily, sunlight within 30 min — fixes mood swings at the source.
- No phone 1 hour before bed. Physical alarm clock. Phone in another room.

**The Rebel Frame:**
> "Nobody is forcing me. I chose this. The system is my tool, not my master. I can break it anytime — but I choose not to."
>

### Distraction Shields That Actually Work (Q17)

1. **Verbal Commitment** — before opening any distraction, say out loud: *"I am about to waste time. I am choosing this."* The act of speaking kills the autopilot.
2. **My own Paper Contracts** — write and sign daily agreements with myself about what I will and won't do. Accountability through ceremony.
3. **No WiFi Environments** — prepare work offline, go to a place with no internet (offline café, balcony in airplane mode, park with no signal). If there's nothing to escape to, you can't escape.

## Bridge the Knowing-Doing Gap (Practical Exercises)

### Emotional Regulation (Daily)

- **Trigger Log:** Every anger/frustration moment — note (1) What happened? (2) Body feeling? (3) Self-talk? (4) Action taken? Builds pause between trigger and reaction.
- **5-5-5 Reset:** When triggered — name 5 things you see, 5 you hear, 5 you feel. Forces brain out of fight-or-flight.
- **Delayed Response Rule:** When someone disagrees, wait 5 seconds. Take 3 breaths. Then decide.

### For Boring Tasks (Editing, Taxes, Forms)

- **5-Minute Commit:** "I'll do 5 minutes. If I still hate it, I can stop." Usually keeps going.
- **Body Doubling:** Work alongside someone (even virtually). Presence reduces resistance.
- **Environment Shift:** Move to a different location. Brain associates spaces with states.

### Self-Confidence & Doubt Elimination

- **The Evidence Log (daily, 3 min):** Write 3 things you did today that old you couldn't have done. Tiny is fine. Reprograms identity with proof.
- **"I'll Show You" Visualization (2 min, morning):** See everyone who doubted you watching you succeed. Feel it. Then work. This is fuel.
- **Fear-Setting (weekly, 15 min):** For the thing you're avoiding — worst case? Likelihood? What would I do? Cost of inaction in 1 year, 5 years?

### Self-Therapy Practices

- **Inner Dialogue Switch:** Pre-write replacement scripts. Memorize them:
  - Premiere crashes: "FUCK this" → *"Error in system. Find another way."*
  - Mistake: "You're stupid" → *"Feedback. What can I learn?"*
  - Disagreement: "They're idiots" → *"Interesting. What's their angle?"*
- **Parts Mapping (monthly, 20 min):** Interview your inner parts — The Fighter, The Achiever, The Rebel, The Perfectionist, The Escapist. What does each want? Fear? Need from you?

### Deep Work Protocol

- **90-Minute Sprint:** One task. No phone. No notifications. Wandering mind? Note it on paper, return. 20 min break after. Max 2 sprints/day.
- **Pre-Session Hack:** Say "I notice I'm resisting. That's fine. I'm starting anyway." Set 5-min timer. After 5 min, you're in flow.
- **Weekly Integration (Sunday, 30 min):** What did I learn about myself? What pattern beat me / did I beat? ONE thing to change next week. ONE thing I will absolutely NOT allow to happen again.
