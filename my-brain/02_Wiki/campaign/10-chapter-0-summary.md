## My Origin Story

Born in Poland. First 6 years were pure adventure — forest exploration, building things, feeling free. Then school started, and so did the complexity.

**The Coach Wound:** Soviet-era acrobatics coach who deducted points from a perfect score — never praised, only pointed out mistakes. Resulted in a **mistake-focus mindset** that persists today.

**The Ridicule Wound:** Made creative videos about acrobatics. Gym friends mocked them. Outside the bubble, everyone loved them. The message: *"Exposing yourself gets you punished. Better to hide."*

**The Father Wound:** Watched dad's businesses collapse 2-3 times from corruption, not lack of skill. Absorbed: *"Doing business is dangerous. They will get you."*

**The Identity Split:** Local celebrity on the outside, outcast on the inside. Two identities. Never quite belonging.
