# Commitment: Discipline, Environment & Energy

## Overview

Covers self-discipline habits, mental conditioning, environment reset, energy management, and making retreat impossible. Supports the $17K/mo goal from skill 06.

---

## Step 1: Self-Discipline Habit

### Q1 — Keystone Habit
> **Keeping sleeping schedule.** ~8h sleep, consistent. Everything else depends on this.

### Q2 — Single-Handling
> Close off distractions. Travel/train/plane environments for deep focus.

### Q3 — Accountability System
> Small steps, remove distractions, visible progress.

### Q4 — When Motivation Fades
> 5-minute rule, switch task, identity anchor.

---

## Step 2: Build Mental Fortress

### Q5 — Mental Conditioning Practices (Daily Stack)

| Practice | When | Why |
|----------|------|-----|
| **Sleep routine** | Nightly, 21-22 screen off → pool → bed | Keystone — fixes mood swings at source |
| **Morning meditation** | 10 min AM | Builds pause between trigger and reaction |
| **Evidence Log** | 3 min EOD | "3 things I did today old-me couldn't." Reprograms identity with proof |

**Situational:**
- Cold showers — when you need a reset
- "I'll Show You" visualization — before high-resistance tasks (recording, posting)
- Trigger Log — when anger hits

### Q6 — Identity Affirmations

> "I am someone who **stays calm always.** "
>
> "I am someone who **delivers despite conditions.** "
>
> "I am someone who **expresses himself freely.** "

### Q7 — Handling Doubt

When doubt hits, in order:
1. **Evidence Log glance** — proof I deliver
2. **Rebel Frame** — "I chose this. The doubt is old programming, not truth."
3. **Fear-Setting quick check** — worst case? so what?

**Anchor reminder:**
> "Doubt is the echo of an old self. I'm not that person anymore."

### Q8 — Daily Discomforts

1. **Cold shower** (AM) — physical resilience
2. **1 micro-post** (one sentence of value) — systematic desensitization for visibility wound
