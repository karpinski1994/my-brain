# LinkedIn Video Strategy

## Summary

LinkedIn is pushing video as a strategic priority, with the new Brand Link monetization system. The key is expert-led educational videos (not viral entertainment), optimized hooks, and proper caption formatting.

## The Video Opportunity

LinkedIn video is at a blue ocean moment similar to carousels 2 years ago. The platform is manually boosting videos that meet specific criteria.

### What LinkedIn Wants
- **Expert-led educational content** — tutorials, industry breakdowns, deep dives
- **Subject matter expertise** matching your profile
- NOT viral trends, motivational fluff, or TikTok-style entertainment

### Brand Link (Creator Monetization)
LinkedIn's new monetization system (beta, US only). Instead of ad revenue split, LinkedIn connects high-value creators with brands looking to spend marketing budget on expert-led video content.

## Video Structure

### Hook (First 3 seconds)
5 high-retention hook styles:
1. **Paradox** — "I regret improving myself"
2. **Bold How-To** — "Here's how to never waste time networking again"
3. **Counter-Intuitive Claim** — "Cold showers are ruining your mornings"
4. **Emotional Bait** — "I was broke last year, but today I'm here"
5. **Number** — "In 14 days, this changed everything"

### Body (Educate)
Keep it stupidly simple (KISS). Two structures:
- **Step-by-step**: Step 1 (mistake) → Step 2 (breakdown) → Step 3 (fix)
- **Problem-Mistake-Fix**: Most people do X → The mistake → Here's how to fix it

### Ending (Viewer-Centered CTA)
- "Try this for 7 days and watch what happens"
- "Follow for more shortcuts like this"
- "Apply it and thank me later"

## Filming & Editing

- No studio needed — daylight or $20 ring light
- Teleprompter optional; bullet points on phone work fine
- Editing: CapCut for silence removal, auto-subtitles, background music
- Phone-filmed content works as well as $2K camera content

## Caption Strategy

- Do NOT copy-paste the script
- Caption hook should complement video hook (not repeat it)
- Keep under 800 characters
- 8-10 words max per line, spaced lines
- Bullet points as summary of the video
- Visual formatting matters
