# ICP Voice of Customer

## Summary

Exact phrases, metaphors, and emotional language extracted from 32 sales calls. Use these verbatim in LinkedIn posts, Facebook content, ads, and outreach to sound like a developer, not a marketer.

## Top 5 "Copy Gold" Phrases

1. **"The Nine Circles of Hell"** — Dante-esque metaphor for the job interview process
2. **"I'm a Good Trade for the Company"** — guilt of being high-value but low-cost
3. **"Technical Debt is Killing my Passion"** — boredom of maintenance work
4. **"I'm Just a Technician to Them"** — loss of status as an engineer
5. **"Screaming into the LinkedIn Void"** — sending 100+ apps with zero feedback

## Pain Phrases (Use as Hooks)

### The Struggle
- "I'm just living paycheck to paycheck"
- "I feel like I'm a try-hard but I don't pass the first exam"
- "I'm tired of being dman (f\*\*\*ed) by outsourcing"
- "I don't want to be just a number"
- "I'm earning less now than I was 15 years ago"
- "My resume says 20 years, but I feel like I just graduated"
- "I have to be extraordinary just to beat local developers"
- "I don't have any bank balance"

### The Market
- "All those job offers come from companies taking the job from a different company and outsourcing"
- "The intermediary was there just to pass money from one hand to another"
- "Even though the salary is good, I can't save money"
- "For Europe it's much less because this is the market"

### The Internal Struggle
- "I have this kind of mind barrier where just we're just in that"
- "Maybe I'm not good enough for this kind of opportunity"
- "I don't even know what the problem anymore"
- "I want to give my best, although I'm aware that sometimes it may not be enough"

## Desire Phrases (Use as Aspirational Hooks)

### The Dream
- "I want to double my income"
- "I want to move from operational to strategic thinking"
- "I want to stop exchanging time for money"
- "I want a bulletproof mentality"
- "I want to sniper-shot the companies, not go in like a kombajn (combine harvester)"

### The Lifestyle
- "Relocate to Spain or Thailand on a US salary without needing a visa"
- "I want to pass more time with my son"
- "Freedom to organize my time so nobody suffers"

## Buying Triggers

### Trigger 1: "Middleman Realization" (Anger/Unfairness)
"You are doing 100% of the work, but your agency is taking 60% of the check."
Evidence: John and Jose realized US clients paid $120k+, they saw $60k.

### Trigger 2: "Seniority Gap" (Status/Security)
"You have 15 years of experience, but you're terrified of a LeetCode test."
Evidence: Carlos and Bastian were "scared" of technical interviews despite being veterans.

### Trigger 3: "Time Freedom" (Freedom/Love)
"Earn in 20 hours what you currently earn in 60. Get your life back."
Evidence: John and Jose wanted time with children and wives.

### Trigger 4: "AI Urgency" (Fear of Obsolescence)
"AI isn't taking your job, but a programmer who knows how to use AI will."
Evidence: Multiple prospects mentioned learning AI.

## Content Writing Rules

1. **No corporate speak.** Use "middleman tax," "paycheck to paycheck," "LeetCode anxiety."
2. **The 20 years vs. Junior paradox.** "15 years of experience but still sweating when the screen share starts? We need to talk."
3. **The Agency Robbery angle.** "Your US client thinks they're paying you $80/hr. You're only seeing $30. Where is the rest going?"
4. **The Lifestyle Close.** "Earn US rates from anywhere. Relocate to Spain or Thailand, no visa needed."
