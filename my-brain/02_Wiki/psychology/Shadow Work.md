# Shadow Work

## Summary

Active practice of inner child healing, hypnotherapy, and auto-hypnosis to surface and process unconscious material. 27+ traumas identified. The goal is to heal root causes rather than manage symptoms.

## Key Ideas

- **Methods tried**: inner child healing, hypnotherapy, self-hypnosis, auto-hypnosis, psychoeducation
- **Result**: surface-level awareness of traumas but difficulty integrating — "I know what's wrong and what to do but still can't act"
- **Paradox**: intellectual understanding without behavioral change → frustration loop
- **Therapist experience**: hit-and-miss — hypnotherapist was helpful, psychologist/couples therapy felt ineffective
- **Current stance**: skeptical of therapy as fix, leaning toward self-directed shadow work
