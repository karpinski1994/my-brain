# Psychological Portrait

## Summary

A comprehensive self-analysis spanning ADHD screening, MBTI personality evolution (2019-2025), childhood wounds, relationship patterns, and entrepreneurial blocks. The portrait reveals a highly adaptive personality oscillating between creative enthusiasm (ENFP), strategic leadership (ENTJ), disciplined structure (ISTJ), and analytical reflection (INTP).

## Key Ideas

- **ADHD self-report score: 3/6** — just below clinical threshold, but significant tendencies toward inattention, procrastination, and emotional dysregulation
- **MBTI evolution**: ENFP (2019) → ENTJ (2021) → ISTJ (2022) → INTP (2025) — a journey from extraverted feeling to introverted thinking
- **Core conflict**: heart vs. head, freedom vs. structure, passion vs. discipline
- **Deep need**: freedom, meaning, relationships — despite external adaptations
- **Primary blocks**: fear of judgment (from childhood mockery), fear of "sticking out" (from father's caution), perfectionism, dopamine addiction (gaming)
