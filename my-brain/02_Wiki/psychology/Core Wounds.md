# Core Wounds

## Summary

27 identified traumatic experiences surfaced through self-hypnosis. The core wounds center around ridicule for creative expression, a strict/traumatic coaching relationship, maternal neglect, and paternal fear-based modeling around business and visibility.

## Key Ideas

- **The Ridicule Wound**: Childhood videos were mocked by close friends despite strangers liking them → "showing myself = pain"
- **The Coach Wound**: Acrobatics coach (second father figure) was very strict, often yelled → authority = threat
- **The Village Wound**: Mocked for being from a village, for being "too philosophical," for being eloquent → being different = isolation
- **The Mother Wound**: Mother was unemployed, slept late, didn't pack lunch → "I am not worthy of care" projection onto partner
- **The Father Wound**: Father feared "sticking out" due to tax office → success + visibility = danger
- **School Rebellion**: Skipped classes because education system felt obsolete → authority structures = waste of potential

## Interpretation

These wounds form two clusters:
1. **Visibility = Danger** (ridicule + father + coach): blocks marketing, posting, self-promotion
2. **Not Good Enough** (village + mother + coach): blocks belief in product, self-worth, taking money

Both clusters feed into the [[ADHD Profile]] procrastination loop and [[Relationship Patterns]].
