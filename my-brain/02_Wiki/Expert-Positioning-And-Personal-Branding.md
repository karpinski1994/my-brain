# Expert Positioning & Personal Branding

## Summary

Personal branding is reputation at scale. The 2026 LinkedIn shift rewards domain experts over content creators. The strategy: micro-niche down, lead with proof, build authority through repetition, and make your personal brand algorithm-proof.

## The Blueprint

The foundational sequence: **Content → Distribution → Sales**. In the attention economy, products aren't hard — distribution is. Eyeballs sell at mass scale.

### The 3-Layer Approach
1. **Unfair advantages** — what makes you unique (clients worked with, unique systems, personal experiences)
2. **Market demand** — what people actually need right now
3. **Personal brand** — the vehicle that combines both

## Micro-Niche Strategy

### Niche Down, Then Niche Up
- Start hyper-specific: "Email automations for e-commerce brands doing $1M+/year"
- Dominate that micro-niche
- Expand once established
- Alex Hormozi example: dominated fitness → expanded to business → became "the niche"

### Rule of One
One problem, one solution, one offer, one topic. Scattered content = scattered audience. Focused content = authority.

## Expert-Led Content (2026+)

LinkedIn's algorithm shift: rewarding domain authorities, not content creators.

### Proof-First Content Strategy
- **80% tactical** — based on lived experience, screenshots, case studies before/after
- **20% behind-the-scenes** — you doing the work
- Every post must answer: "How do I know this person has actually done this?"
- "Here's what I did and here's what happened" beats "Here's what I learned"

### Thought Leadership Ads
Boost organic posts as ads — they're indistinguishable from regular content. Take proven content, promote it to thousands more without creating new content.

## Personal Branding Principles

### Writing Is the Skill
Writing (texting, video scripts, emails, posts, comments) is the foundational skill of personal branding. It sets you apart more than any other skill.

### The Hook → Meat → CTA Structure
Every post needs:
1. **Hook** — grabs attention
2. **Meat** — engaging points and breakdowns
3. **CTA** — tells them what to do next

### Psychology Over Tactics
Understanding WHY people consume content (to be educated, inspired, entertained) matters more than any single tactic. Appeal to pain points, not just features.

### Transform in Public
Documenting your journey from beginner to expert builds a cult-like following. People connect with the come-up story. Share what you know, learn more, write about it, iterate.

### Building an Algorithm-Proof Brand
- Viral creators: interesting content, but cover their name and you don't know who they are
- Lifetime creators: recognizable from a mile off — even when educating, people stay for THEM
- Use repetition, personal stories, unique frameworks, your face/photo

## Networking as Leverage

### Coffee Chat Strategy
In the first months: pack your calendar with 50-min coffee chats with people at all levels (behind you, alongside you, ahead of you).
- Plants seeds in people's minds
- Leads to referrals, support, and opportunities
- One mentor found through a comment → connection → coffee chat → years of guidance

### Being an Asset
The highest-converting pitch: solve a problem for free with precise execution. Send a Loom video, a resource, a PDF — no ask, no price. Makes you unforgettable.
