# AI Content Workflow

## Summary

A curated 6-tool AI content stack that enables creating viral LinkedIn content in minutes. The key is using AI for ideation and first drafts while keeping the human voice in final edits.

## The 6-Tool Stack

### 1. Opus (Content Ideation)
Taps into YouTube's viral content database. Input a keyword → get outlier ideas that have already worked in long-form. Use these as blueprints for short-form content across platforms.
- Best for: finding proven content angles
- Key prompt: "Analyze this and give me 10 similar viral hook ideas"

### 2. Cleo (AI Writing)
Personal AI writing assistant that learns your voice, tone, and business. Pulls from viral frameworks but writes like YOU.
- Asks onboarding questions to understand your style
- Can import ChatGPT/Claude memories for accuracy
- Built by analyzing top creators' writing styles
- Generates variations of specific sentences
- Key: it writes in YOUR voice, not generic AI

### 3. Microsoft Copilot (GPT-4 Creative Writing)
Set to "more creative" mode. Better than standard ChatGPT for generating LinkedIn hooks because it uses real-time web context.
- Prompt 1: "Analyze this [viral content] and give me 10 similar viral hook ideas"
- Prompt 2: "Finish writing this post in the style and tone of [creator]. Keep each sentence 8-10 words max, add a space after each period, keep under 30 lines."

### 4. ChatGPT Projects (Journal & Organization)
Use projects as organized journals. Voice-to-text notes that are stored by topic. When creating content, reference project notes for authentic stories and ideas.
- Setup: one project per topic (journal, business, content)
- Voice-to-text for hands-free idea capture
- Key prompt: "Take these voice notes, don't generate anything — just organize them into coherent thoughts"

### 5. Poppy AI (Long-Form Scripts)
Upload examples of your writing style → generate long-form scripts, emails, SOPs.
- Key prompt: "Based on what you know about me, how I speak, and the topics I write about, make a 20-page SOP for my script writer"
- Use 40-60% of output; edit the rest

### 6. CapCut (Video Editing)
Auto-subtitles, silence removal, format conversion, background music. For short-form video content.

## Prompting Framework

### Content Pillars Generation
```
Based on [context about you], give me 3-5 specific content pillar themes that will help me become an authority in my niche while being professional given my background.
```

### Hook Generation
```
Based on these [viral hooks], create me 5 viral LinkedIn post hook ideas for each. Make the hook follow this format: a bold statement no more than 8 words in sentence one, a second line that acts as a rehook challenging that statement.
```

### Full Post Generation
```
Write me a viral LinkedIn post that is 800 characters long. Sentences short and concise, no longer than 8 words per sentence. Switch up formatting, easy to read. No hashtags. Follow this format: [example posts from creators you like].
```

## Key Principles

- AI is for first drafts, not final output
- The prompt is the product — invest time in crafting good prompts
- Context is everything: feed AI your actual experiences, client calls, and voice notes
- Simpler words, not complex — remove AI's natural 5th-grade vocabulary floor
- Write like you explain to a friend (FaceTime call, not sales pitch)
- Add descriptive and emotional words instead of long tangents
- Optimize for mobile readability (short lines, spacing)

## Content Ideation Sources

- **Twitter** — millions of pre-validated pieces of content in every industry
- **YouTube** — hardest platform to crack; proven ideas transfer easily to LinkedIn
- **Your own client calls** — transcribe with Opus, extract problems and solutions
- **Swipe files** — save viral hooks, posts, and frameworks from creators you admire
