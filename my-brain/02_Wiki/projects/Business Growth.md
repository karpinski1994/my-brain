# Business Growth

## Summary

A mentoring business helping advanced programmers find better jobs, earn more, and grow. The product works (2 satisfied clients with testimonials) but marketing is blocked by fear of visibility, imposter syndrome, and toxic beliefs about selling.

## Key Ideas

- **Product**: course + mentoring for programmers to land B2B roles in US/UK
- **Validation**: 2 paying clients, positive testimonials
- **Block**: fear of posting on LinkedIn (where colleagues/friends follow), fear of being "exposed"
- **Toxic belief**: "selling knowledge is a scam when info is free online"
- **Competing desire**: want to pivot to something more adventurous (travel, nature, physical work)
- **Financial pressure**: runway running out, considering "normal" job

## Current Strategy

- Document don't create (share journey, not polished content)
- Build in public
- Use INTP strengths: analytical deep-dives, not hype
