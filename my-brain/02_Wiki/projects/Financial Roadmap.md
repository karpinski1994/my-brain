## Revenue → Savings Mechanics

```
Savings Growth = (Monthly Revenue - Monthly Burn) × Months

At $3K/mo revenue: $1K/mo savings → $12K/year
At $5K/mo revenue: $3K/mo savings → $36K/year
At $8K/mo revenue: $6K/mo savings → $72K/year
At $12K/mo revenue: $10K/mo savings → $120K/year
At $20K/mo revenue: $18K/mo savings → $216K/year
```

The game accelerates: each chapter makes the next one faster.
