# Diet for Good Sleep

> Recommendations for sleep-supportive nutrition, considering reflux, gastritis, and IBS.

## Principles

- Light, easily digestible foods for dinner
- Avoid high-fat foods, especially saturated fats
- Eat last meal 2-3 hours before sleep
- No drinks 2-3 hours before sleep

## Recommended Foods (Evening)

| Category | Examples |
|----------|----------|
| Lean meats | Chicken, turkey, fish |
| Legumes | Lentil stew (leczo z soczewicy) |
| Dairy | Cottage cheese with vegetables/fruit |
| Spreads | Homemade hummus on whole-grain bread |
| Grains | Oatmeal with milk/water, dairy, nuts, banana |
| Eggs | Soft-boiled, egg paste |
| Smoothies | Fruit with nuts |

## Recommended Ingredients

- Pumpkin seeds, chia seeds, flaxseed
- Cocoa, walnuts, hazelnuts, almonds
- Kiwi, bananas, avocado
- Spinach

## General Daily Guidelines

- **Fiber**: 25-30g daily (wheat bran 2-3 tbsp = min 30g)
- **Water**: 2 liters daily
- **Bread**: Whole-grain rye, graham rolls, sourdough rye
- **Breakfast**: Whole-grain bread preferred

## Foods to Avoid (Evening)

- Heavy fatty foods
- Raw vegetables (especially for IBS sensitivity)
- High-fiber foods close to bedtime
