# Sleep & Stress

## Current Sleep Pattern

- Sleep time: ~12:00 AM – 8:00 AM
- **Main problem**: Waking up multiple times during the night (not falling asleep initially, though that's now also becoming difficult)
- Occasionally cannot sleep at all — entire night and next day awake
- Has entered a vicious cycle: stress about not sleeping → wakes up more

## Mental Health Context

> Psychiatric symptoms — chronic stress, "stressed non-stop"

- Has tried: meditation, running, healthy eating for 6 months — no improvement
- SPECT scan showed **low frontal cortex activity**
- Cognitive problems: feeling "stuck with my thoughts"
- Some anxiety present, not many depression symptoms
- **High cortisol suspected** → melatonin suppression

## Impact of Poor Sleep

- Joint pain when sleep-deprived
- Irritability
- Memory problems
- Concentration problems
- Occasional loose stools
- Occasional dizziness (rare)
- Upper back pain (between shoulder blades)

## Treatment History

| Treatment | Result |
|-----------|--------|
| Lifestyle changes (meditation, running, diet — 6 months) | No improvement |
| Hydroxyzine 40mg | No effect, felt groggy |
| Stilnox (Zolpidem) | Helped temporarily |
| Duloxetine + Pregabalin (current, prescribed by psychiatrist) | Calmer, more energy, but mental numbness, memory/concentration issues |
