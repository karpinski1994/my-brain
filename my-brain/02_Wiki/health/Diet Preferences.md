# Diet Preferences

## Core Requirements

- **Easy & fast** to prepare
- **Good for stress and sleep disorders**
- **International cuisine** — Asian, Mexican (spicy), Italian

## Foods I Like

### Proteins

| Food | Notes |
|------|-------|
| Poultry (chicken, turkey) | All |
| Fish (especially salmon) | All |
| Shrimp | |
| Tuna | Canned in water |
| Beef steak | |

### Fats

- Butter
- Olive oil
- Coconut milk

### Vegetables

Bell peppers, carrots, bamboo shoots, chili peppers, scallions, lettuce, cucumber, jalapeños, tomato, dill, broccoli, ginger, parsley, mung bean sprouts, radish, eggplant, zucchini

### Fruits

All, especially: strawberries, bananas, oranges, kiwi, watermelon, lychee, blueberries, blackberries

### Carbohydrates

| Food | Notes |
|------|-------|
| White rice | Preferred |
| Yellow rice | |
| White bread | |
| Spaghetti pasta | |
| Rice noodles | |

### Dairy

- Blue cheese (Brie, Gouda, Edam)
- Yellow cheese
- Coconut milk
- Mozzarella
- Natural yogurt
- Milk 1.5%

### Condiments & Extras

- Soy sauce
- Mustard
- Honey
- Rice vinegar
- Wine vinegar
- Red wine (in dishes)
- Sesame seeds
- Pickles: gherkins, pickled peppers, pickled jalapeños in olive oil

### Nuts & Seeds

- Walnuts
- Brazil nuts
- Almonds
- Chia seeds

## Foods I Dislike / Cannot Eat

- **Gas-producing foods**: beans (kidney, white), onions, pumpkin, peas, chickpeas
- **Pork** (any)
- **Avocado** (not really)
- **Oatmeal and porridge** (unless blended as milkshake)
- **Eggs** (dislike)
- **Offal/organ meats** (liver, ham, etc.)

## Favorite Recipes

### 1. Shake
> Milk, natural yogurt, strawberries, banana, blackberries — blended

### 2. Pasta Suta (Tomato Pasta)
> ~8 plum tomatoes, half large onion, 2-3 garlic cloves, oregano, basil, olive oil, rosemary, 2 bay leaves, sea salt, red wine

### 3. Spaghetti with Blueberries
> Canned tomatoes, 2-3 garlic cloves, handful of blueberries, a few anchovies, a bit of onion

### 4. Spaghetti with Soy Sauce
> Soy sauce, spaghetti, sun-dried tomatoes in oil, tuna, garlic, spices

### 5. Thai Soup
> Chicken breast (~500g), chicken broth (400-500ml), coconut milk (400ml), red bell pepper, onion, red curry paste (4 tsp), garlic (4 cloves), fresh ginger, lemongrass paste, olive oil, fish sauce, brown sugar, lime juice, scallions, fresh cilantro, fresh basil, salt, pepper, rice noodles (100g dry)

### 6. Quick Chinese
> Rice + chicken + mixed Asian frozen vegetables + soy sauce

### 7. Broccoli Soup
> Potatoes, broccoli, carrot, chicken broth cube, bread, pumpkin seeds, mozzarella — blend
