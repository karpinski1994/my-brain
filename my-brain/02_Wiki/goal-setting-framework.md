# Goal Setting Framework

A combined framework from Brian Tracy's 12-step goal-setting method and a personal 12-step life planning system.

## Summary

Goals must be clear, written, measurable, and have deadlines. The framework combines vision (dream), planning (breaking down into steps), obstacle anticipation (pre-mortem), and daily execution systems. Plans are tools, not cages — they must be adapted to your nature.

## Key Claims

- Only 3% of adults have written goals; everyone else works for them (source: `01_Raw/12-Step-Goal-Setting-Guide.md`)
- Each minute spent in planning saves 10 minutes in execution (1000% ROI) (source: `01_Raw/12-Step-Goal-Setting-Guide.md`)
- Your weakest key skill sets the height of your income and success (source: `01_Raw/12-Step-Goal-Setting-Guide.md`)
- 80% of constraints holding you back are internal (lack of skill, knowledge), only 20% external (source: `01_Raw/12-Step-Goal-Setting-Guide.md`)
- A plan is a tool, not a cage — play around your nature, don't copy others' routines (source: `01_Raw/worksheet-forming-plan.md`)
- You can't steer a car that isn't moving — start before you feel ready (source: `01_Raw/worksheet-forming-plan.md`)

## The 12 Steps (Brian Tracy)

1. Decide exactly what you want
2. Write it down
3. Set a deadline with sub-deadlines
4. Identify obstacles (Theory of Constraints)
5. Identify knowledge and skills needed
6. Identify people whose help you need
7. List everything you'll have to do
8. Organize list by sequence and priority
9. Make a plan
10. Select #1 most important task daily
11. Develop self-discipline (single-handling)
12. Practice visualization

## The Personal 12-Step System

1. Clarify your dream (vision lock)
2. Define it clearly (SMART goals)
3. Identify core actions (daily/weekly habits)
4. Create daily execution plan
5. Run a pre-mortem (why might you fail?)
6. Obstacle elimination plan
7. Build your mental fortress
8. Install daily discipline blocks
9. Do weekly reviews
10. Reset your environment
11. Guard your energy and identity
12. Make your mission unavoidable
