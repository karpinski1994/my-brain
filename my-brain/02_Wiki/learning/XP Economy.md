# XP Economy

## Summary

A weighted XP system where harder/scarier tasks award more points. Designed to reward action over perfection and to make high-resistance tasks (marketing, posting) feel worth doing.

## Key Ideas

- **Weighted by resistance**: the more you want to avoid it, the more XP it's worth
- **XP multipliers**: 3x for tasks involving publishing, 2x for tasks involving recording, 1x for maintenance
- **Categories**: Business (marketing, sales, product), Health (exercise, diet, sleep), Learning (courses, reading), Relationship (quality time, therapy work)
- **Level up**: every 100 XP → new level
- **Streak bonus**: consecutive days of activity multiply XP

## Sample Values

| Task | Base XP | Multiplier | Total |
|------|---------|------------|-------|
| Post on LinkedIn | 10 | 3x | 30 |
| Record a video | 15 | 2x | 30 |
| Reply to comments | 5 | 2x | 10 |
| Exercise | 10 | 1x | 10 |
| Read 30 min | 5 | 1x | 5 |
| Quality time with R | 10 | 1x | 10 |
