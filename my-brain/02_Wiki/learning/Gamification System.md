## Summary

A personalized gamification framework designed to overcome procrastination (especially LoL addiction) and build sustainable motivation. Based on Octalysis framework, Self-Determination Theory, and tailored to INTP personality type.

## Key Ideas

- **Core problem**: anxiety-driven avoidance → dopamine-seeking (LoL) → guilt → more avoidance
- **Solution**: replace guilt-driven system with XP-driven reward system
- **Personality fit**: INTP-T responds to narrative reframing, autonomy, mastery, and novelty — not rigid schedules or shame
- **Narrative framing**: "Secret Identity" reframe — the hero's journey, not the slave's grind
- **Environment design**: add friction to escapes (block LoL), reduce friction for work (post templates, place computer outside)
- **White Hat vs Black Hat**: use White Hat (accomplishment, empowerment, meaning) to attract, manage Black Hat (avoidance, scarcity, unpredictability) traps
