# 30-Day Onboarding Plan

> Transition from anxiety-driven procrastination to gameful automaticity.

## Week 1: Tutorial & Environment Design
**Goal:** Reduce activation energy, overcome friction of newness

### Days 1–3: Physical & Digital Armor
- Post-it note over TV/monitor power button: "Entry Cost: 5-Minute Caption Polish"
- Set up Forest or Cold Turkey to block gaming sites during "Quest Window" (9:00 AM – 11:00 AM)

### Days 4–7: The "First Strike" Buff
- Non-negotiable 15-minute ritual at 9:00 AM
- Write one sentence for LinkedIn or edit 1 minute of video subtitles
- "First Strike" XP Multiplier (2x) for that task

## Week 2: Building Momentum & Progress Visualization
**Goal:** Transition from fear-based motivation to Core Drive 2 (Development & Accomplishment)

### Days 8–10: Weighted XP
- High-resistance tasks (subtitle editing) assigned 3x XP
- Track visible progress
