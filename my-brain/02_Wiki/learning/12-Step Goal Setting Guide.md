# 12-Step Goal Setting Guide

> Brian Tracy's goal-setting framework. Reference for structured goal definition.

## The 12 Steps

1. **Decide exactly what you want** — Idealize without limitations
2. **Write it down** — A goal not written is just a wish
3. **Set a deadline** — Sub-deadlines for larger goals
4. **Make a list** — Everything you can think of that you'll need to do
5. **Organize into a plan** — Sequence, prioritize
6. **Take action immediately** — Do something, anything
7. **Do something every day** — Consistent forward momentum
8. **Think about your goals daily** — Morning visualization
9. **Refuse to make excuses** — 99% is about persistence
10. **Review your progress weekly** — Learn, adjust, improve
11. **Anticipate obstacles** — Pre-mortem planning
12. **Never give up** — Resilience is the key
