# My Gamification Approach

> Tailored strategy based on INTP-T "Logician" personality profile. Shift from function-focused design to human-focused RPG.

## Core Problems Addressed

- 10–12h daily gaming (League of Legends) vs 15–30 min work
- Deep need for autonomy and creativity (Core Drive 3)
- Paralyzed by anxiety and lack of Epic Meaning (Core Drive 1)

## Strategy Pillars

### 1. Reframe Tasks as Narrative Quests
- Stop calling it "subtitle editing" → "Decoding Ancient Communication Logs"
- Speedrunning for Flow: set timer, beat personal best

### 2. High-Resistance Point Economy
- Weight XP by cognitive resistance, not just volume
- High-resistance tasks = 3x XP

### 3. Environment Design Over Willpower
- Block distractions (Cold Turkey/Forest)
- Design "Entry Cost" friction
- First Strike Ritual: 15 min at 9:00 AM

### 4. Black Hat → White Hat Progression
- Week 1: Use urgency/scarcity (Black Hat) to start
- Week 2+: Transition to accomplishment/empowerment (White Hat)
