# Flow State

## Summary

The optimal state of consciousness where challenge meets skill, time disappears, and work feels effortless. Central to the gamification system — the goal is to make business work as engaging as gaming.

## Key Ideas

- **Conditions for flow**: clear goals, immediate feedback, challenge-skill balance
- **My flow triggers**: novelty, progress visibility, working outside/nature, spontaneous inspiration (not deadlines)
- **Anti-flow triggers**: operational drudgery, forced schedules, broken tools, admin tasks
- **Gaming hijack**: LoL provides perfect flow — clear goals (win), instant feedback (KDA), escalating challenge. Business must compete.
- **Strategy**: structure business tasks to mimic game loops — clear objectives, visible progress bars (XP), variable rewards

## The Architecture vs Bricklayer Concept

"You are an architect, not a bricklayer" — focus on design, vision, and creative work. Outsource or batch the bricklaying (operations, admin, editing).
