# Rewards Shop

## Summary

A three-tier reward system where XP earned from tasks can be spent on guilt-free treats. The key insight: remove guilt from "wasted" time by earning the right to leisure.

## Key Ideas

- **Tier 1 — Minor (10-30 XP)**: Guilt-free gaming session (1h), takeout coffee, episode of favorite show
- **Tier 2 — Moderate (50-80 XP)**: Takeout dinner, cinema trip, buying a game
- **Tier 3 — Major (100+ XP)**: Weekend trip, gear purchase, guilt-free full gaming day

## Philosophy

The goal is not to eliminate League of Legends — it's to make it a *reward* rather than an *escape*. When gaming is earned, there's no guilt loop. When it's an escape, there's guilt → more escape.
