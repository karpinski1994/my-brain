# Copywriting Templates & Swipe File

## Summary

A collection of copywriting templates, headline formulas, hook techniques, and email strategies. Built from swipe files and notes on top copywriters (Sabri Suby, Adrian Kołodziej, Gary Halbert, Sam Ovens, Russell Brunson).

## Blog Article Structure

1. **Headline** — Start here. Use proven templates (see below). Sabri Suby is a good reference.
2. **Introduction** — Why it matters to the reader, what they'll get, summarize what you'll cover.
3. **Body** — One thought per 1-2 sentences, hyperlinks for authority, subhead sections, images/infographics, secondary keywords.
4. **CTA** — Clear call to action.

## Headline Templates

### "How To" Headlines
- `How To Get _________`
  - How To Get A Better Score On Your Next PT Test
- `How To _________ In As Little As _________`
  - How To Get Rid Of Acne In As Little As 24 Hours
- `How To _________ In As Little As _________ . . . even if you _________!`
  - How To Pass Your PT Test In As Little As 2 Weeks . . . even if you can't do a single pullup right now!
- `How Every _________ Can _________`
  - How Every Teenager With Pimples Can Have Clearer Skin Fast!

### "Ways To" Headlines (numbered lists)
- `5 Quick And Easy Ways To _________`
- `3 Fast Ways To Get _________ And Avoid _________`
- `5 Quick And Easy Ways To Get _________ . . . Even If _________!`

Odd numbers (3,5,7,9) carry more credibility.

### "Mistakes" Headlines
- `Which Of These _________ Mistakes Will You Make?`
- `_________ Mistakes All _________ Need To Avoid!`
- `_________ Mistakes Every _________ Needs To Avoid!`

### "Warning" Headlines
- `WARNING: Here's What Every _________ Needs To Know About _________`
- `WARNING: Don't Even Think About Trying To _________ Until You Read This`

Warning headlines grab attention from everyone (like an animal alarm call). Don't cry wolf — use them intelligently.

### Other Proven Templates
- `Here's The Perfect Solution If You Want _________`
- `Here's The Perfect Solution If You Want _________ (even if _________)`
- `My Proven "_________" Method To _________`

### Headline Formula
**Big Idea + Emotion + Specific Result or Threat**

Spend 50% of your writing time on the headline. The headline starts the entire sales process. Test new headlines against the current one — changes can produce 500% improvement in sales.

## Hook / Lead Techniques

The hook is the first few lines of your copy (sometimes includes headline). Its job is to grab attention so they keep reading.

### 1. Shocking Statement
A short, snappy sentence with a fact, stat, idea, tragedy, or shocking number.

### 2. Funny Joke / Comparison
- Funny comparisons: "This is what most people's funnels look like" (with a photo of shit)
- Relatable absurdity: photo of Kaczyński with a cat → "Want to end up like him?"
- Use comparisons tied to their problem/situation or their desire/goal

### 3. Bold Promise
Talk about what they desire and how reading this text will give it to them.

### 4. Open Loop
"In this way you'll achieve X — read on to find out how."

### 5. Imagination
"Imagine that… (now)… (then)… (bridge)"

### 6. Direct Question
"Do you want to achieve XYZ? Read more."

### 7. One-Sentence Persuasion
"People will do anything for those who encourage their dreams, justify their failures, allay their fears, confirm their suspicions, and help them throw rocks at their enemies."

### 8. Storytelling
People love stories. Great stories:
- Break patterns — unpredictable
- Heroes and villains (you're the hero, who's the villain?)
- Conflict/risk/adventure/intrigue
- Use data: "2 out of 10 men struggle with dating"
- Tell a story of a customer, mentor, or yourself — relatable to the email topic
- Talk about your success, struggles, pivotal moments, discoveries

### 9. Use Someone Else's Story
- Leverage attention of a person your prospect cares about (e.g., "Tears of Jeff Bezos" by Sabri)
- Client story: someone who was in their situation and now is where they want to be

### 10. Images & GIFs
Use images and GIFs that grab your dream customer's attention (in-feed photos, engaging visuals).

### 11. Short Emails with Value + Quick CTA

### Hook Formula
**An unlikely character + timing + results**
or
**A result without pain + timing**

### Hook Examples
- "Create and publish your outrageously profitable book in just three hours without writing a single word."
- "Lose all the weight you want in the next thirty days without diet or exercise."
- "Part-time chicken rancher loses 30-pounds in two months thanks to the unlikely combo of Oprah Winfrey and a former U.S. Navy Seal."
- "Former janitor goes from bankrupt to paid-off house in eighteen months with profits from his e-book business."
- "Meet Bob. Bob used a 1600-year-old secret to save his marriage, and we'll show you how."
- "Ex-pizza delivery guy shows you a weird trick to become a bestselling author in a weekend."
- "Rookie realtor takes 52 listings first year in the business using this 15th century discovery."
- "How I used a simple hack to go from bankrupt and living in a trailer park to become a successful real estate investor."
- "From dead broke with a heart condition and living in a trailer park with no heat and a leaky roof to internet millionaire."

### Email Spam Strategies
- Segmentation
- Daily emails
- Emails from different accounts
- Attention-grabbing subject lines (A/B test for highest open rate)
- Pre-write 200 emails ahead
- If someone doesn't open, resend with a different subject line
