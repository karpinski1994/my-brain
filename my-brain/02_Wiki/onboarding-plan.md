# Onboarding Plan

A 30-day plan to transition from "anxiety-driven procrastination" to "Gameful Automaticity" using the Octalysis framework and the user's psychological portrait.

## Summary

A structured 4-week onboarding: Week 1 reduces activation energy with temporary Black Hat scaffolding, Week 2 builds momentum with progress visualization, Week 3 introduces variable rewards for creativity, and Week 4 solidifies identity through Boss Battles.

## Week 1: Tutorial & Environment Design
- Physical & digital "armor" (post-it notes, Forest app blocking LoL)
- "First Strike" buff: non-negotiable 15-min ritual at 9 AM
- Reward: 2x XP multiplier for task done first thing

## Week 2: Momentum & Progress Visualization
- Weighted XP: subtitle editing = 3x XP
- Progress bar filling visibly as boring work is completed
- Narrative reframing: "marketing" → "Documenting the Guild's Progress"

## Week 3: Creative Empowerment & Variable Rewards
- D20 Strategy: roll 20-sided die after completing a task
- 1-15: standard XP, 16-19: bonus Dopamine Dollar, 20: Legendary Reward
- Speedrunning: set timer to beat personal record

## Week 4: Identity Reinforcement & Boss Battles
- stickK commitment contract for high stakes
- First "Boss Battle": posting a video testimonial or opinion on LinkedIn
- "Just One" technique: click Post and close browser immediately
- Day 30: "Level Up Ceremony" — XP total, progress review, major reward

## Daily Loop
- Morning Buff: High-protein breakfast (no simple carbs)
- The Quest: 30-60 minutes of "Documenting the Journey"
- XP Logging: immediate visual feedback
- Reward Window: spend earned XP on gaming/relaxation after quest
