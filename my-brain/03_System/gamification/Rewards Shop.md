# Rewards Shop

> *The goal is not to eliminate escapes — it's to make them rewards for progress, not retreats from fear.*

---

## Currency

- **XP** earned from quests — spend on daily/weekly rewards
- **Milestones** earned from revenue & savings targets — unlock major life rewards
- 1 XP = 1 point of reward value
- XP not refundable once spent

---

## D20 Critical Hit Table

Roll when you complete a **boring or dreaded task**:

| Roll | Outcome | Effect |
|------|---------|--------|
| 1 | Critical Fail | Lose 5 XP |
| 2–5 | Common | No bonus |
| 6–10 | Uncommon | +2 XP |
| 11–15 | Rare | +5 XP |
| 16–19 | Epic | Free Tier 1 reward |
| 20 | Legendary | Free Tier 2 reward + +10 XP |

### When to Roll
- Admin / accounting
- Cold outreach / DMs
- Any task avoided for 3+ days
- Editing captions
- Paying taxes

## Rules

1. **Earn first, spend later** — no XP debt
2. Log it: `[date] Spent [XP] on [reward]` in [[XP Tracker]]
3. Keep 10 XP buffer
4. XP carries across levels
5. Milestone rewards are **non-negotiable** — once unlocked, take them
