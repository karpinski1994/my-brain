# Rewards Shop

> *The goal is not to eliminate League of Legends — it's to make it a reward rather than an escape.*

## Currency

- **XP** earned from quests and tasks
- **1 XP = 1 point** of reward value
- XP is non-refundable once spent
- Rewards must be claimed within 7 days of earning

## D20 Critical Hit Table

Roll a physical D20 (or digital) when you complete a **boring or dreaded task**:

| Roll | Outcome | Effect |
|------|---------|--------|
| 1 | **Critical Fail** | Lose 5 XP — task needs rework |
| 2–5 | **Common** | No bonus XP |
| 6–10 | **Uncommon** | +2 bonus XP |
| 11–15 | **Rare** | +5 bonus XP |
| 16–19 | **Epic** | Free Tier 1 reward |
| 20 | **Legendary** | Free Tier 2 reward + +10 XP |

### When to Roll
- Editing captions
- Paying bills / accounting
- Any admin task
- Cold outreach / DMs
- Any task you've been avoiding for 3+ days

---

## Spending Rules

1. **Earn first, spend later** — no XP debt
2. **Log it**: `[date] Spent [XP] on [reward]` in XP Tracker
3. **Balance check**: Keep at least 10 XP buffer for emergencies
4. **Carry over**: Unused XP carries across levels (no reset on level up)
