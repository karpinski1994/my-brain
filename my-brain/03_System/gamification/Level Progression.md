# Level Progression

## Formula

```
TotalXP = 100 × (Level - 1)^1.5
```

Early levels come fast (immediate feedback). Later levels require more investment (sustained progression).

## Perk Details

- **Minor rewards**: 1h gaming, takeout coffee, episode of show
- **Moderate rewards**: Takeout dinner, cinema, new game
- **Major rewards**: Weekend trip, gear purchase
- **Legendary rewards**: Full guilt-free gaming day, tech upgrade
- **Streak protection**: 1 missed day per week doesn't reset streak
- **Guilt reduction**: All leisure activities cost 50% less psychological weight
